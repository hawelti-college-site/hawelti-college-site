About the College

Hawelti Axum Polytechnic College is committed to delivering high-quality TVET (Technical and Vocational Education and Training). Our curriculum is industry-aligned, our instructors are experienced practitioners, and we focus on practical skills, entrepreneurship, and community development.

Short-Term Programs & Partnerships

We run short-term and modular programs in partnership with NGOs, city administration, and local communities. Example offerings include:

  • Solar PV installation & maintenance (partner: local NGOs)
  • Basic ICT and digital skills for youth (partner: city administration)
  • Small-scale irrigation & community farming techniques (partners: community groups)
  • Entrepreneurship bootcamps & business clinics (partners: incubation hubs)
